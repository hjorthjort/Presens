How does it work?

By analyzing real-world data we show you the best recommendations for you.

Time

We use your time to see give you the most popular showings at that time and day of the week.

Weather

We look at the weather of your location, to give you the perfect show or movie for a rainy or a sunny day.

Twitter trending

We analyze twitter trends at your location, to make sure you don't miss out on what's hot and what everybody is talking about.

Historical data

We look at what other people have been looking at during similar conditions, to make sure that our recommendations are the best for you.
